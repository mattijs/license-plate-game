Plate Spotter

A road-trip spotting game for Colorado license plates.

How to play

Tap a plate card whenever you spot that plate on the road. It turns green to mark it as found. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser — no account needed.

Sharing a game

Every game has a unique ID shown at the bottom of the screen. Share the link with your travel companions so everyone tracks the same road trip. Note that each player's progress is stored locally on their own device — sharing a link doesn't sync spotted plates between devices.

The plates

All 218 Colorado license plates are sourced directly from the Colorado DMV, covering Standard, Special Interest, Military, and Alumni categories.
